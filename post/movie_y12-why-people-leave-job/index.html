[分享] 年輕工作者離職的最大原因

Sat, Oct 13, 2012

閱讀全文 http://www.businessinsider.com/why-young-employees-quit-their-jobs-2012-9 最大的兩個原因是： 1. 沒有學到足夠的東西。（覺得停滯不前） 2. 沒有受到足夠的重視，跟導師帶領，或職業訓練。 （Quote: finds that a loss of training opportunities and a lack of mentors in the workplace are two of the biggest reasons why young workers leave.) 當然有的時候是因為錢，但是大多時候前並不是第一的主因： ‘Sometimes it’s because the job isn’t what they thought it was going to be. More often, they weren’t getting the personal attention, the mentoring, the coaching, the training they wanted.’”
